Robotic Hand Project

Thu, Jun 9, 2016

Robotic hand projects led my way to this field. They were my initial interests and I am still interested.

I started my robitics journey from junior year when I was in Tsinghua University, Beijing. One day I saw this cool robotic hand project in Dr. Wenzeng Zhang’s lab and asked him if I could work with him. He said yes, and then for the next two years, I worked with him and learned a lot from him.

After undergraduate, I decided to continue to graduate study. I had the honor to work with Dr. Peter Allen in his Robotics Lab at Columbia University. I spent almost two years there and it was exciting and very fun because we got to build a robotics hand from scratch.
